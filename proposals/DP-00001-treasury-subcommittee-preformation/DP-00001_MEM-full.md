## 1. Purpose

This Resolution does two things:

1. Releases a capped legal/compliance budget from the DAO treasury (**on-chain, immediate upon execution**).  

2. Appoints Treasury Subcommittee **Designates** for pre-formation readiness work (**no delegated treasury authority**).

---

## 2. Operative rule

This is **normative** text proposed for adoption by Governance System. If (and only if) it is adopted and the Execution Bundle executes successfully, the operative clauses below are effective as stated.

---

## 3. Definitions

1. **"Company"** means SOLOMON DAO LLC, pending formation.

2. **"Designates"** means the persons named in §6, appointed for readiness only.

3. **"Execution Bundle"** means the on-chain transactions that implement the on-chain portions of this Resolution.

4. **"Governance System"** means the on-chain mechanism by which this Resolution is adopted and executed.

5. **"Legal Budget"** means the capped amount released under §5.

6. **"Legal Budget Wallet"** means the destination wallet/vault specified in §5.2.

7. **"Treasury Subcommittee"** means the post-formation company treasury subcommittee contemplated by the Company Agreement framework.

8. **"Undertaking"** means the Treasury Subcommittee confidentiality undertaking.

---

## 4. Hard constraint: no delegated treasury authority granted here

**RESOLVED:** This Resolution does **not** grant delegated authority to act as the Treasury Subcommittee and does not authorize any Designate to control or transact from any DAO treasury account or any Company treasury account.

**RESOLVED:** Delegated authority for Treasury Subcommittee operations may attach only via a **post-formation activation resolution** (intended: DP-00003 or equivalent) that:
- confirms formation effectiveness, and
- designates the relevant company treasury account(s) / vault(s), and
- sets mechanical constraints (multisig threshold, caps, allowlists, revocation, reporting).

**RESOLVED:** Designates may be removed or replaced by subsequent governance action.

### 6.2 Permitted scope (readiness only)

**RESOLVED:** Prior to post-formation activation, Designates are authorized only to perform readiness work, including:
- drafting proposed treasury operating policy/guardrails for later adoption,
- preparing the second multisig/vault configuration plan (signers, threshold, roles),
- preparing allowlists/caps/revocation procedures and incident response playbooks,
- preparing due diligence checklists and provider shortlists,
- preparing a phased execution plan for later approval.

### 6.3 Undertaking requirement (confidential access gate)

**RESOLVED:** Each Designate must execute the Undertaking as a condition precedent to receiving any Confidential Information or security-sensitive operational details.

### 6.4 No agency; no spokesperson

**RESOLVED:** Nothing in this Resolution creates any agency, partnership, fiduciary relationship, or spokesperson authority. No Designate may represent themselves as able to bind the Company or control DAO or Company treasury assets.

## 7. Execution Bundle scope limitation (binding)

**RESOLVED:** The Execution Bundle for DP-00001 shall include only:
- the Legal Budget transfer in §5.2.

**RESOLVED:** The Execution Bundle for DP-00001 shall not include:
- any buyback operations,
- any governance threshold updates,
- any creation/designation of company treasury accounts/vaults,
- any delegation of treasury signing authority to Designates,
- any treasury deployment transfers beyond the Legal Budget.
