# Website Registry of SOLOMON DAO LLC

**Status:** Pre-formation (executed by Organizer; not adopted by on-chain vote)

**Effectiveness:** This Registry takes effect on the Effective Date of the Company's Certificate of Formation.

**SECONDARY INSTRUMENT.** This Website Registry is a secondary instrument of the Company Agreement.

**Not a Registrar Registry.** This Website Registry is an internal governance instrument of the Company. It is not the registry maintained by the Registrar of Corporations, the Marshall Islands DAO LLC Registry, or the IRI Registry. This Registry is maintained by the Company for purposes of DAO Act §102(p) and does not replace or purport to be any Registrar-maintained registry.

This Registry designates the Company's official online venues for purposes of §102(p) of the Decentralized Autonomous Organizations Act 2022 (as amended).

Updates to this Register take effect when approved by Resolution, or (where permitted) provisionally upon adoption pursuant to authority delegated under the Founding Charter (acting within its mandate), provided the update is recorded in the SOP Registry and submitted for ratification in accordance with the Founding Charter.

---

## Intellectual Property Firewall

**"Protocol"** in this Registry has the same meaning as **"Protocol IP"** as defined in Operating Agreement §2.1(17) - design, documentation, code, repositories, and other intellectual property. It does not mean an operating business, service, issuer, custodian, counterparty, or other person or entity.

Listing a domain or URL in this Registry designates it as an official online venue of the Company only to the extent expressly stated in Part I. Any domain, URL, repository, communication channel, or other property not listed in Part I is not an official Company venue. Nothing in this Registry implies ownership, operation, control, administration, hosting, key custody, or compliance responsibility by the Company for any property.

## Amendment

This Registry is a Secondary Instrument and may be amended by Resolution in accordance with §6.5(3) of the Operating Agreement. Any material change to the structure, format, or governing rules of this Registry requires Resolution.

---

## Registry Maintenance and Filing (DAO Act §102(p))

This Registry must be maintained current for purposes of annual filings required by the DAO Act. The Head Steward may update this Registry for filing purposes only to reflect: (1) technical corrections; (2) changes previously approved by Resolution; and (3) changes required to maintain accuracy of filed information, in each case in accordance with §6.6 of the Operating Agreement.

The Head Steward may not: (1) materially alter the structure or format of this Registry; (2) add or remove entries except as approved by Resolution or required for accuracy; (3) misrepresent the contents of this Registry; or (4) use filing authority to expand the scope or authority of any instrument. Any material change to this Registry requires Resolution.

---

## Record-Keeping Updates

Entries may be added, removed, corrected, or refreshed as record-keeping updates in accordance with §6.6 and §6.7 of the Operating Agreement, provided such updates do not materially alter the structure, format, or governing rules of this Registry and do not expand the scope, authority, or legal effect of any instrument.

Emergency record-keeping updates required to mitigate a security incident, exploit risk, or imminent harm to Company Treasury Accounts may be made and recorded as soon as reasonably practicable in accordance with §6.7(3), but only to the extent consistent with §6.6.

Any record-keeping update must be recorded in the SOP Registry and submitted for ratification as required by §6.7.

Record-keeping updates do not, by themselves, create authority to operate any website, forum, repository, or communication channel, or to assume compliance responsibility for any such property.

---

## Relationship to Registrar Filings

The Representative Agent is responsible for ensuring that official online venue information filed with the Registrar is consistent with this Registry.
