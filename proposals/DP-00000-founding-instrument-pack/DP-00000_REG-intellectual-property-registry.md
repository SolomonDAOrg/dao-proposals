# Intellectual Property Registry of SOLOMON DAO LLC

**Status:** Pre-formation (executed by Organizer; not adopted by on-chain vote)

**Effectiveness:** This Registry takes effect on the Effective Date of the Company's Certificate of Formation.

**SECONDARY INSTRUMENT.** This Intellectual Property Registry is a secondary instrument of the Company Agreement.

**Not a Registrar Registry.** This Intellectual Property Registry is an internal governance instrument of the Company. It is not the registry maintained by the Registrar of Corporations, the Marshall Islands DAO LLC Registry, or the IRI Registry. This Registry is maintained by the Company and does not replace or purport to be any Registrar-maintained registry.

This Registry is the canonical list of Protocol IP and Marks owned or controlled by the Company.

Updates to this Register take effect when approved by Resolution, or (where permitted) provisionally upon adoption pursuant to authority delegated under the Founding Charter (acting within its mandate), provided the update is recorded in the SOP Registry and submitted for ratification in accordance with the Founding Charter.

---

## Intellectual Property Firewall

**"Protocol"** in this Registry has the same meaning as **"Protocol IP"** as defined in Operating Agreement §2.1(17) - design, documentation, code, repositories, and other intellectual property. It does not mean an operating business, service, issuer, custodian, counterparty, or other person or entity.

This Registry enumerates Protocol IP only. It does not enumerate or imply ownership of, custody over, or operational control over any deployments, operational stacks, infrastructure, third-party services, or third-party assets.

---

## Licensing of Protocol IP

Protocol IP enumerated in this Registry may be licensed to third parties in accordance with the IP License Framework. Each license is recorded in the IP License & Assignment Register and includes a Licensee Declaration in which the Licensee sets out, in its own words and at its sole discretion, the parameters of its intended use.

The Company does not direct, supervise, approve, or control any Licensee's use of Protocol IP or the content of any Licensee Declaration. The enumeration of Protocol IP in this Registry does not imply any operational role by the Company.
