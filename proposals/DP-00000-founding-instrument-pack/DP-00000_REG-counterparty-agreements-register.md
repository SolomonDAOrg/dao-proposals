# Counterparty Agreements Register of SOLOMON DAO LLC

**Status:** Pre-formation (executed by Organizer; not adopted by on-chain vote)

**Effectiveness:** This Register takes effect on the Effective Date of the Company's Certificate of Formation.

**SECONDARY INSTRUMENT.** This Counterparty Agreements Register is a secondary instrument of the Company Agreement.

**Not a Registrar Registry.** This Register is an internal governance instrument of the Company. It is not the registry maintained by the Registrar of Corporations, the Marshall Islands DAO LLC Registry, or the IRI Registry. This Register is maintained by the Company for internal governance traceability and does not replace or purport to be any Registrar-maintained registry.

This Register is the canonical index of agreements the Company enters into with external parties (including amendments, statements of work, side letters, accession instruments, joinders, and terminations).

Updates to this Register take effect when approved by Resolution, or (where permitted) provisionally upon adoption pursuant to authority delegated under the Founding Charter (acting within its mandate), provided the update is recorded in the SOP Registry and submitted for ratification in accordance with the Founding Charter.

---

## Intellectual Property Firewall

**"Protocol"** in this Register has the same meaning as **"Protocol IP"** as defined in Operating Agreement §2.1(17) - design, documentation, code, repositories, and other intellectual property. It does not mean an operating business, service, issuer, custodian, counterparty, or other person or entity.

This Register records agreements as legal instruments and metadata only. Recording an agreement (including by identifier, path, hash commitment, or reference) does not, by itself, create or imply any operational role, compliance responsibility, agency, fiduciary duty, partnership, joint venture, custody, or control by the Company, and does not make any counterparty conduct attributable to the Company.

For avoidance of doubt, no agreement indexed in this Register is incorporated into the Company Agreement or any Protocol IP instrument by implication or course of dealing.

---

## Separation; No Conflation

This Register is for record-keeping and governance traceability only.

Recording an agreement in this Register:

1. does not amend, supplement, or modify the IP License Framework, any license of Protocol IP, the Intellectual Property Registry, or the IP License & Assignment Register, and does not incorporate any such instrument by reference, except where the Company has expressly adopted such amendment in the manner required by the Company Agreement and recorded it in the SOP Registry;
2. does not create any agency, partnership, joint venture, fiduciary, employment, or representative relationship between the Company and any counterparty; and
3. does not confer any third-party beneficiary rights.

For avoidance of doubt, a counterparty may be both (a) a party to one or more agreements indexed here and (b) a licensee of Protocol IP, but those are separate arrangements and must not be characterized as a single integrated arrangement by implication or course of dealing.

## Recording and Confidential Particulars

Agreements and related instruments may be recorded as (a) a "Public Record" and (b) "Confidential Particulars".

The Public Record contains non-confidential metadata and a cryptographic hash commitment (e.g., SHA-256) to the then-current "Agreement Package" (being the executed agreement and any referenced schedules, amendments, and side letters).

A hash commitment is for provenance and integrity only and does not, by itself, disclose, publish, or grant rights in the Agreement Package.

Confidential Particulars are maintained off-chain and, to the extent stored in the SOP Registry, are maintained only in hashed and/or encrypted form and must be convertible to legible written form within a reasonable time where required by applicable law.

## Record-Keeping Updates

Entries may be added, removed, corrected, or refreshed as record-keeping updates under delegated authority in accordance with §6.7 of the Operating Agreement, provided such updates do not materially alter the structure, format, or governing rules of this Register. Any such update must be recorded in the SOP Registry and submitted for ratification as required by §6.7.
