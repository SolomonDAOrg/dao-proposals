# IP License & Assignment Register of SOLOMON DAO LLC

**Status:** Pre-formation (executed by Organizer; not adopted by on-chain vote)

**Effectiveness:** This Register takes effect on the Effective Date of the Company's Certificate of Formation.

**SECONDARY INSTRUMENT.** This IP License & Assignment Register is a secondary instrument of the Company Agreement.

**Not a Registrar Registry.** This IP License & Assignment Register is an internal governance instrument of the Company. It is not the registry maintained by the Registrar of Corporations, the Marshall Islands DAO LLC Registry, or the IRI Registry. This Register is maintained by the Company and does not replace or purport to be any Registrar-maintained registry.

This Register is the canonical record of IP license grants and IP assignments relating to Protocol IP.

Updates to this Register take effect when approved by Resolution, or (where permitted) provisionally upon adoption pursuant to authority delegated under the Founding Charter (acting within its mandate), provided the update is recorded in the SOP Registry and submitted for ratification in accordance with the Founding Charter.

---

## Intellectual Property Firewall

**"Protocol"** in this Register has the same meaning as **"Protocol IP"** as defined in Operating Agreement §2.1(17) - design, documentation, code, repositories, and other intellectual property. It does not mean an operating business, service, issuer, custodian, counterparty, or other person or entity.

This Register concerns Protocol IP only. It does not license, approve, supervise, or regulate any Licensee's business.

---

## Licensee Declaration Records

Licensee Declarations are self-declarations made by each Licensee in connection with acceptance of a License. The Company does not dictate, direct, approve, or supervise the content of any Licensee Declaration. Each Licensee determines its own Declaration at its sole discretion.

The recording of a Licensee Declaration in this Register:

1. does not constitute Company approval or endorsement of the Declaration's content;
2. does not constitute Company direction or control of the Licensee's business;
3. does not warrant the adequacy, accuracy, or legal effect of the Declaration; and
4. is for record-keeping purposes only.

Differences between Licensee Declarations of different Licensees reflect the independent decisions of independent entities, not direction by the Company.

---
